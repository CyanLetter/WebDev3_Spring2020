Positioning

Relative, Absolute, and when to use them

From the Edge In

If you are going to break the borders of your container... make another container.

Relative

  • Required for Absolute to work correctly
  • DO NOT use 'top', 'right', etc. for layout

Absolute

  • Use top, right, bottom, and left to position
  • Relative to the last non-static parent

Absolute and Creating Components

  • Use it to layer stuff over other stuff
  • Use in containers with other relative content
  • ...Or ones with known dimensions
